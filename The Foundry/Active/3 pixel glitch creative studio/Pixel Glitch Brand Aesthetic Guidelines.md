## Typography
- Headings: VCR OSD Mono, Silkscreen, or Pixel Emulator
- Body text: Space Grotesk, IBM Plex Sans, or Euclid Circular B
- Accent text: JetBrains Mono or Fira Code for technical elements

## Visual Elements
- Grid systems with strategic breaks
- Pixel-based illustrations with deliberate imperfections
- Geometric shapes creating optical illusions
- Texture overlays mimicking analog distortion
- Clean lines with digital artifacts

## Composition Principles
- Asymmetrical balance with intentional disruptions
- Strategic negative space creating shapes/illusions
- Layering of analog and digital elements
- Subtle motion effects for digital assets

## Photography Style
- High contrast imagery
- Selective chromatic aberration
- Double exposure techniques
- Film grain or analog noise elements
- Digital/analog hybrid processing

## Logo Concept

- Contains optical illusion element
