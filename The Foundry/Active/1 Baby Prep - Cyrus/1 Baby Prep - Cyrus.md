## 📦 Pre-Hospital Departure Checklist

**When labor starts, complete these tasks:**

### 🔔 Notify
- [ ] Text Verenice (Doula): 510-604-3301 — include contraction timing
- [ ] Call hospital Labor & Delivery to let them know you're coming
- [ ] Notify family/support people

### 📊 Track Contractions
- [ ] Start timing contractions (Start to Start)
	- **Frequency** — how far apart
	- **Duration** — how long each lasts
	- **Intensity** — how strong
- [ ] Use the **5-1-1 Rule** to decide when to go:
	- Contractions **5** minutes apart
	- Lasting **1** minute each
	- For **1** hour
- [ ] Check in 30 minutes before heading out if everything feels stable

### ⚠️ Go Immediately If
- [ ] Heavy bloody show (pad of blood)
- [ ] Water breaks and fluid is brown/green (not clear)
- [ ] Strong downward pressure that won't ease

### 🏠 Before Leaving the House
- [ ] Grab **Small Carry-On** (clothes, chargers)
- [ ] Grab **Blue Duffel** (documents, labor essentials, comfort items)
- [ ] Grab **Empty Second Duffel** (for bringing home items)
- [ ] Confirm car seat is installed and secure
- [ ] Hydrate and eat a light snack
- [ ] Unplug appliances, lock up
- [ ] Bring insurance cards and ID
- [ ] Bring birth plan copies
- [ ] Bring noise-canceling headphones + music playlists loaded
- [ ] Bring iPad or Apple TV for video

### 🧘 During Early Labor at Home
- [ ] Stay home as long as possible if all is well
- [ ] Move your body — walk, change positions
- [ ] Deep breaths, guided meditations during waves of calm
- [ ] Lower shoulders, release tension
- [ ] Rest and sleep if you can
- [ ] Light snacks, stay hydrated

2. **Pushing & Birth** (2-3 hours) - Most active phase, very focused, skin-to-skin immediately after

5. **Burying Placenta** - Many cultures honor this; common to discard in US
