## 🌿 Environment Comfort

- [ ] **Lighting & Mood**
  - [ ] Phone with brightness control app (or manual controls)
  - [ ] Portable LED lights or string lights (if hospital allows)
  - [ ] Small flashlight with soft setting
  - [ ] Eye mask or sunglasses for bright periods

- [ ] **Sound & Music**
  - [ ] Headphones or wireless earbuds
  - [ ] Curated labor playlist downloaded
  - [ ] White noise or nature sounds app installed
  - [ ] Speaker for ambient music (check hospital policy first)

- [ ] **Temperature & Comfort**
  - [ ] Extra blanket from home (with hospital approval)
  - [ ] Layers you can remove/add easily
  - [ ] Warm socks with grip bottoms
  - [ ] Small fan or hand fan

- [ ] **Scents & Sensory**
  - [ ] Essential oil roller (check hospital policy on aromatherapy)
  - [ ] Unscented lotion if preferred
  - [ ] Lip balm

- [ ] **Personal Touches**
  - [ ] Photo of loved ones (partner, River, etc.)
  - [ ] Small personal item from home
  - [ ] Favorite candle-scent room spray (hospital-safe)
  - [ ] Massage tools or tennis ball for pressure points

- [ ] **Post-Labor Comfort**
  - [ ] Special robe or nightgown (easy to open for skin-to-skin)
  - [ ] Comfortable pillow from home in colored pillowcase (to distinguish from hospital)
  - [ ] Slippers with grip soles
