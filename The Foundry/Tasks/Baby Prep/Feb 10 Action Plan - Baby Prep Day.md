## 💡 Pro Tips for Today

**Car Seat Installation:**
- Install 2-3 weeks early = NOW is the time
- Center back seat is safest position
- Professional inspection gives peace of mind

**Hospital Bag Packing:**
- Use colored pillowcase on home pillow (distinguish from hospital linens)
- Bring empty duffel to carry home hospital supplies/gifts
- Hospital provides: diapers, wipes, basic toiletries, pads, disposable underwear

**Pediatrician Selection:**
- Alta Bates privileges = doctor can examine baby at hospital
- Without privileges = staff pediatrician does exam, you see your doctor after discharge
- First appointment typically 3-5 days after birth

**Birth Plan:**
- Can change your mind about anything during labor
- Use BRAIN method for decisions: Benefits, Risks, Alternatives, Intuition, No/Not Now
- Bring 3-4 copies for shift changes
