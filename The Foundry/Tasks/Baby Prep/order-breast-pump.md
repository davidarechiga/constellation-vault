## Insurance Coverage Notes

From Alta Bates class notes:
> "You can order your breast pump early and they will send it to you a month or more before your due date with one baby, by week 32 with twins. You contact your insurance company and they will give you the information about the company that will fulfill your order and what pumps they cover under your plan."

## Steps to Order

1. **Call Insurance** - Ask for durable medical equipment (DME) department
2. **Get Vendor Info** - Insurance will provide approved vendor contact
3. **Choose Model** - Compare covered options (hospital-grade vs. standard)
4. **Submit Order** - Usually done online or by phone with vendor
5. **Confirm Delivery** - Allow 1-2 weeks for shipping

## Popular Covered Models

Common insurance-covered pumps:
- Spectra S1 / S2
- Medela Pump in Style
- Motif Luna
- Lansinoh Smartpump

Research reviews on which model best fits needs (portability, battery, suction strength, noise level).
