Body Paragraphs

### Topic Sentence
This is the Reason why
only 1 reason should be given

**A BAD topic sentence:** "The US needs to combat climate change by phasing out gas-powered cars because **politicians aren't doing enough**." (This is too broad. What is **one specific thing** they are doing/not doing that is worrisome?)

The US needs to combat climate change by phasing out gas powered cars because

### Analysis/Explanation

Introduce evidence

### The Significance
who will be impacted?
what is a consequence or two of it
does this relate to something else that you or your reader care about
