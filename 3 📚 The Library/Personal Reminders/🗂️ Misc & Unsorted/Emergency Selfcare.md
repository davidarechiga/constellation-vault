# Emergency Selfcare

## **Taste**
• Your favorite tea
• A delicious chocolate bar
• Some yummy mints
• That slightly-harder-to-find-in-stores candy you really love

## **Touch**
• A sheet mask
• A face refresher spray
• A pretty rock or crystal
• A container of Pinch Me Therapy Dough ([$24](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B076DYVK2Q?tag=bfrachel-20&ascsubtag=4872003%2C4%2C11%2Cm%2C0%2C0%2Cgoogle%2C776%3A1%3B962%3A1%3B901%3A2%3B900%3A2%3B974%3A2%3B994%3A3%2C0%2C0%2C0&ie=UTF8&qid=1532447724&sr=8-4&keywords=pinch%252Bme%252Btherapy%252Bdough) for a large container on Amazon) or a container of Play-Doh
• A fidget spinner
• A mini bottle of lotion or body oil

## **Smell**
• A couple of [essential oils](https://www.buzzfeed.com/tomvellner/how-to-use-essential-oils) (we like the [Vitruvi](https://vitruvi.com/) ones shown above)
• A mini candle

## **See**
• A picture of your pet
• Photos of friends/family
• A list of things you like about yourself or compliments you’ve received

## **Hear**
• A list of songs to play that will help lift your spirits (or a few different ~mood~ playlists)
• A mantra you can repeat out loud to yourself
