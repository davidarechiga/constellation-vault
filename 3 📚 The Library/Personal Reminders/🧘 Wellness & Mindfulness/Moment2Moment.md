# Moment2Moment

Summary

A morning framework, that encompasses meditation, journaling, and stretching to maintain a sense of mental balance and keeping clear direction.

Meditation 
Journaling 
Stretching 

The morning starts with a glass of water and a quick 5 to 15-minute meditation. This is a good time to let any negative self-talk flow right past. After the meditation, a quick, five-minute journal entry to jot down any reflections from yesterday or intentions for the day ahead.
