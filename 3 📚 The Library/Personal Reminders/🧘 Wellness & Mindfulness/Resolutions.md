The week between christmas and new years is becoming my week of reflection for the previous year. Finding out what works and what is detrimental is key. It’s looking like each blog post is more personal than the previous and this is no different. Why I have never made new years resolutions I will never know. I tend to push away common social practices in an attempt to think different but most of the time it just holds me back from becoming who I am destined to be. I am stubborn and quiet like my father. The past couple days, I’ve been struggling with my sense of purpose, my sense of individuality and what it is that I bring to the table of life. What I am struggling to find is consistency in self discipline. I have acknowledged this many times in my life and oddly enough the problem stays. Making 2016 the year of consistency is my most important resolution. The only way I see progress happening is in consistency and sticking to a plan. Making goals has always been a challenge.
