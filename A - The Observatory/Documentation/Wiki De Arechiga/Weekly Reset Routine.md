# Weekly Reset Routine

## Overview

_A weekly household reset to keep things from spiraling. Pick a day (Sunday works well) and knock it out together._

---

## Checklist

### Home
- [ ] Laundry (wash, dry, fold, put away)
- [ ] Vacuum / sweep all rooms
- [ ] Wipe kitchen counters and appliances
- [ ] Clean bathrooms (quick clean)
- [ ] Take out trash and recycling
- [ ] Tidy living areas

### Admin
- [ ] Process mail (see [[Mail & Package Handling]])
- [ ] Check calendar for the week ahead
- [ ] Review grocery needs → make list
- [ ] Pay any pending bills

### Personal
- [ ] Weekly review (see [[Weekly Review Template]])
- [ ] Plan meals for the week (see [[Meal Planning System]])
- [ ] Restock essentials (diapers, dog food, etc.)

---

## Tips & Notes

- Do it together — divide and conquer
- Put on music or a podcast to make it enjoyable
- Target 60-90 minutes total
