# Kitchen Deep Clean Procedure

## Overview

_A thorough kitchen clean beyond the daily wipe-down. Do this monthly or before/after big cooking sessions._

---

## Checklist

- [ ] Clear and wipe all countertops
- [ ] Clean stovetop and burner grates
- [ ] Wipe down oven exterior
- [ ] Clean inside microwave
- [ ] Wipe cabinet fronts
- [ ] Clean sink and faucet
- [ ] Empty and wipe fridge shelves
- [ ] Take out trash and recycling
- [ ] Sweep and mop floor
- [ ] Clean small appliances (toaster, coffee maker)
- [ ] Check for expired food
