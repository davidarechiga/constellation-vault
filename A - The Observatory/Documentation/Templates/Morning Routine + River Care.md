# Morning Routine + River Care

**Frequency:** Daily (Mon-Sun)
**Time:** 7:00-8:00 AM
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes

## Daily Morning Checklist

- [ ] **Light candle + gratitude practice** (5 min)
  - Light candle as ritual opener
  - Think of 3 things grateful for today
  - Set intention for the day
- [ ] **Feed River + fresh water** (3 min)
  - Fill food bowl with proper portion
  - Refill water bowl with fresh water
- [ ] **Take River outside** (10-15 min)
  - Morning walk for bathroom break
  - Brief exercise and sniffing time
- [ ] **River training exercises** (5-10 min)
  - Practice heel position commands
  - Work on attention/focus training
  - High energy, brisk-paced practice
- [ ] **Morning meditation** (10-15 min)
  - Mindfulness breathing practice
  - Optional: Mindfulness Birthing Class breathing exercises

**Why matters:** This routine anchors the day with intention and ensures River's needs are met before work starts. Establishes consistency and sets positive tone. Combines self-care (gratitude, meditation) with pet care responsibilities.

**Done when:** All items checked off, feel centered and ready for day, River exercised and content.

## River Training Focus

From River care notes:

**Current Training Priorities:**
- **Heel position commands** - Walk at proper pace beside you
- **Attention/focus training** - Get River's attention, maintain energy
- **Brisk-paced walks** - Keep energy up, reset when River isn't paying attention
- **Less about perfect form, more about engagement** - Energy and connection over rigid structure

## Tips for Consistency

✅ **Success Strategies:**
- Wake up same time daily (even weekends)
- Lay out clothes night before
- Keep candle/lighter in obvious spot
- Set out River's food bowl as reminder
- Use morning routine as "no phone" time
- Track in habit tracker for accountability

❌ **Common Pitfalls:**
- Hitting snooze too many times
- Checking phone first thing (disrupts intention)
- Skipping when tired (when it matters most)
- Rushing through meditation (defeats purpose)

---

## Flexibility Guidelines

**Non-negotiable elements:**
- Feed River + take outside (pet depends on you)
- Quick gratitude moment (even 30 seconds)

**Flexible elements:**
- Training length can vary (5-15 min)
- Meditation can be 5-15 min
- Candle ritual can be brief on busy days

**Permission to adapt:** If baby arrives and routine disrupts, simplify to essentials. Return to full routine when life stabilizes.
