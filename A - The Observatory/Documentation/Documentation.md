## Vault Architecture

**ACE Method + Zettelkasten**

| Folder | Role | Contains |
|--------|------|----------|
| **A - The Observatory** | Atlas (Knowledge) | MOCs, Sources, topic notes, reference material |
| **C - Signal Relay** | Calendar (Time) | Daily notes, weekly reviews |
| **E - The Foundry** | Efforts (Action) | Active projects, ongoing life areas, tasks, dashboards |

Notes are found through **links and MOCs**, not folder hierarchies.
