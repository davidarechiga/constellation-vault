The Bishop score is a 0-13 point system that assesses cervical readiness for labor by evaluating five factors:

1. **Cervical dilation** (0-3 points: closed to 5+ cm)
2. **Cervical effacement** (0-3 points: 0-80%+)
3. **Cervical consistency** (0-2 points: firm to soft)
4. **Cervical position** (0-2 points: posterior to anterior)
5. **Fetal station** (0-3 points: -3 to +1/+2)
