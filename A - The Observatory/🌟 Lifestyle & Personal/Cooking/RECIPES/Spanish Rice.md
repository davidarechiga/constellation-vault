<span style="color:#000ff;">Spanish Rice</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice (any long grain will work, I used Basmati)</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">1 1/2 cups chicken broth</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">1 can of</span> <a href="http://www.ro-tel.com/" rel="noopener" class="external-link" target="_blank" style="color:#e4afaff;"><u>Rotel tomatoes</u></a> <span style="color:#000ff;"><i>You can buy this at various spice levels</i></span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">2 tablespoons of melted butter</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">1 teaspoon cumin</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">Optional: You could probably throw in a 4 oz can of chili peppers. I haven't tried this.</span>

<span style="color:#000ff;">Chili Pie</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">Chiles come canned</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">Chicken w/ salt and pepper,</span>
<span style="color:#000ff;">need a pie tin, cheese already grated, Ortega chilies in can. Layer chilies, cheese, meat, chilies, ch</span>

<span style="color:#000ff;">eese meat and chilies, cover with foil and bake at 375 for 45 minutes in oven</span>
