- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon garlic
- 2 chopped garlic cloves
- 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoon chili crisps
- 1/4 cup toasted sesame oil (or olive oil if you can't find but I'm pretty sure we have some in the pantry)
