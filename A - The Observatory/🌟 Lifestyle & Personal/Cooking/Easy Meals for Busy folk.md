# The 7 Best Trader Joe’s Freezer Meals for Busy Families

*By Sheela Prakash — Published January 11, 2026*
*Source: Simply Recipes*

## Key Takeaways

- Mandarin Orange Chicken is a quick, satisfying meal for busy nights
- Frozen pizza is customizable, affordable, and ready in 15 minutes
- Mini tacos with pulled beef and tomatillo salsa make taco night easy and gluten-free

-----

## The 7 Meals

### 1. Mandarin Orange Chicken

- Make two bags for a family of five
- Roast with broccoli florets, serve over rice
- Fan favorite among editors

### 2. Family Size Organic Cheese & Tomato Pizza

- Feeds four, bakes in 15 minutes
- Made in Italy, only **$5.99**
- Takes toppings well — add them to the frozen pizza and bake 1–2 min longer
- Ideas: pepperoni, leftover chicken, sautéed spinach/mushrooms/broccoli

### 3. Mini Beef Tacos

- Filled with pulled beef and roasted tomatillo salsa
- Made with corn tortillas — naturally **gluten-free**
- Chicken version also available
- Cook methods: microwave, air fryer, or oven
- Serve with beans and rice, coleslaw, or Mexican street corn

### 4. Joe’s Diner Mac ’n Cheese

- Cheese blend: cheddar, havarti, gouda, Swiss
- **$2.99 each** — buy two for a family of four
- Add-in ideas: frozen peas, crumbled bacon, roasted veggies, shredded chicken, crumbled sausage

### 5. Fully Cooked Falafel

- 12 falafels per bag, **$3.99**
- Protein-rich, naturally vegan
- Serving ideas:
  - Stuffed in pita with tahini, lettuce, cucumber, tomato
  - Over a simple salad
  - With TJ’s hummus and roasted vegetables

### 6. Chicken Gyoza Potstickers

- Cook methods: air fryer, steam, pan fry, or microwave
- Serve with rice and kimchi
- Also great simmered in chicken broth with frozen veggies as a quick soup

### 7. Outside-In Stuffed Gnocchi

- Potato gnocchi stuffed with tomato sauce and mozzarella
- **$3.69 each** — buy two
- Best cooked on stovetop for crispy exterior
- Finish with olive oil, Parmesan, and wilted baby spinach for a one-pan dinner
