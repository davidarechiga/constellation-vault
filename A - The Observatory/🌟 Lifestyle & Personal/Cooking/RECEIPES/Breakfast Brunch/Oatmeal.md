## Ingredients (3 servings)
- 1½ cups rolled oats
- 3 cups liquid (water/milk)
- Pinch of salt
- 3-4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 scoop cinnamon roll protein powder
- 1 apple, diced

### Optional
- Splash of vanilla extract
- Pinch of nutmeg or cardamom

## Instructions
1. Bring liquid and salt to a boil
2. Add oats, reduce heat to medium
3. Cook 5 minutes, stirring occasionally
4. Stir in brown sugar and protein powder
5. Add diced apples
6. Cook 1-2 more minutes until apples soften slightly
7. Remove from heat, add vanilla if using

## Notes
Adjust consistency with more liquid if needed. Can scale up or down as needed.
