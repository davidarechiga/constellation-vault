# Complete Shopping List for Mediterranean Dinner

## Produce
- 2 heads garlic (one for roasting, plus cloves for other dishes)
- 4 lemons (zest and juice needed for multiple dishes)
- Fresh herbs:
  - Rosemary (fresh)
  - Thyme (fresh)
  - Oregano (fresh) 
  - Parsley (fresh)
  - Mint (fresh)
  - Dill (fresh)
  - Basil (optional, for couscous)
- 2 English cucumbers
- 2 bell peppers (1 red, 1 yellow)
- 1 medium zucchini
- 1 medium red onion
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes

## Dairy & Refrigerated Items
- 16 oz mascarpone cheese
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1 dozen eggs (you'll need 2 whole eggs plus 1 egg white)
- Greek yogurt (1 cup)
- Unsalted butter (need at least 2 tablespoons)
- Feta cheese (1/4 cup crumbled)
