Let's see... Some essentials would be:

Neumann:
KM84/KM184 for small diaphragm mics
U87 for vocals
U67 for vox or guitar
FET 47 for bass or kick
U47 for mono guitars (acoustic)

AKG:
C12 for damn near anything
C12A for toms/overheads
C414 is a studio necessity
C24 is a stereo C12, but super rare 

Sennheiser:
MD421 for electric guitars or toms
MD441 for toms or bass
e609 for electric guitars
e906 also for electrics

Electro Voice RE20 for kick, bass, or edgy vox

Mojave Audio MA200/MA300 which are basically Neumann clones, but cheaper and more accessible 

Shure:
SM7 or SM7B for vox or bass
SM57 for guitars, drums, whatever

Beyer Dynamic:
M88 for bass, kick, or edgy vox/guitars
M201 for snare, guitars (acoustic or electric), bass, percussion, damn near anything else
