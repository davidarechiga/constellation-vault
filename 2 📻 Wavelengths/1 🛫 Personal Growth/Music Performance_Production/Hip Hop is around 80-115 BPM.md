Some of the basic tempo markings

- Larghetto is 60-66 BPM
- Adagio is 66-76 BPM
- Andante is 76-108 BPM
- Moderato is 108-120 BPM
- Allegro is 120-168 BPM
- Presto is 168-200 BPM
- Prestissimo is 200+ BPM
