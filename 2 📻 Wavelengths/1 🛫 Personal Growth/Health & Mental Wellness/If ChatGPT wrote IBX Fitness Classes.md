Absolutely, incorporating timed sets can be effective for maintaining workout intensity. Here's your revised workout plan with timed segments:

**Monday - Upper Body + Cardio**
1. Warm-up: 5 minutes light cardio (jogging in place)
2. Squat + High Pull: 4 minutes, as many reps as possible (AMRAP)
3. Renegade Row + Push-Up: 4 minutes AMRAP
4. Skull Crusher: 3 minutes AMRAP
5. Chest Press: 3 minutes AMRAP
6. Cardio Burst: 5 minutes of jump rope
7. Cool Down: 4 minutes of stretching focusing on upper body

**Tuesday - Lower Body + Cardio**
1. Warm-up: 5 minutes of dynamic stretching (leg swings, bodyweight squats)
2. Kickstand Squat + Kickback: 4 minutes AMRAP
3. Squat + Kneel on Box: 4 minutes AMRAP
4. Lateral Raise: 3 minutes AMRAP
5. Tempo Squats: 3 minutes AMRAP (tempo: 3 seconds down, 1 second up)
6. Cardio Burst: 5 minutes of high knees
7. Cool Down: 4 minutes of lower body stretching

**Wednesday - Core + Cardio**
1. Warm-up: 5 minutes light cardio (elliptical or stationary bike)
2. 4 Round Build (Plank Saws, Tuck-Ins Knee In's with Ball, Bear Shoulder Taps): 4 minutes, rotating each exercise every 30 seconds
3. Cardio Interval: 5 minutes alternating 30 seconds of sprinting with 30 seconds of walking
4. Cool Down: 4 minutes of full body stretching

**Thursday - Full Body + Cardio**
1. Warm-up: 5 minutes of brisk walking
2. Squat + Knee Tap: 4 minutes AMRAP
3. Wiper Curl + Arnold Press: 4 minutes AMRAP
4. Offset Push-Ups: 3 minutes AMRAP
5. Cardio Burst: 5 minutes of burpees
6. Cool Down: 4 minutes of stretching (focus on full body)

**Friday - Lower Body + Cardio**
1. Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cycling
2. Kickstand Squat + Kickback: 4 minutes AMRAP
3. Sumo Squat + Wide-Stance RDL: 4 minutes AMRAP
4. Cardio Burst: 5 minutes stair climbing or step-ups
5. Cool Down: 4 minutes of stretching (focus on the hamstrings and glutes)

**Saturday - Upper Body + Cardio**
1. Warm-up: 5 minutes of arm circles and push-ups
2. 4 Round Build (Skull Crushers Split Stance Row, Tricep Extension): 4 minutes, rotating each exercise every 30 seconds
3. Cardio Interval: 5 minutes alternating 30 seconds of mountain climbers with 30 seconds rest
4. Cool Down: 4 minutes of upper body stretching

Remember to set a timer to keep track of your workout segments, and always work at an intensity that is challenging yet sustainable throughout the set. Take short breaks if needed to maintain good form. Enjoy your workouts and listen to your body!
