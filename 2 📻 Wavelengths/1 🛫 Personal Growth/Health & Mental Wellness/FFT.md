# FFT
### Fucking First Time

### Overview 
It is uncomfortable doing anything for the first time. It sucks for quite awhile. This is a guide to realizing you are doing something for the first time and it’s ok to be easy on yourself. 

## Normalizing/Naming the FFT
- This is exactly how we are suppose to feel
- Can’t draw on history or experience
- Without a net

## Perspective
- Doesn’t mean we suck at everything
- Do you remember when that was so hard?

## Reality Check expectations
- This is a heavier lift than what we expected
- Emotionally
- Physically
- 10x as hard
- Wobbly?
- peak vulnerability
- Not everyone is good at this
- Extra hard on people who are not great with estimating time, eta
- Need to reality check expectations with others
