# Building Your Own Internal Support System

## Creating a Sense of Safety
A support system is suppose to bring you back down to the ground when you are lost in thought. You may be stuck in a past experience you wish had gone differently or anxious about a future experience that you feel unprepared for. These steps will help get you out of your head and into the reality of the present moment.

1. Breathing
	1. Balanced Breathing
	2. Deep Belly Breathing
	3. Straw Breathing
	4. 4-7-8 Breathing
	5. Alternate Nostril Breathing
1. Create a Sensory Experience
	1. See
	2. Smell
	3. Taste
	4. Feel
	5. Listen
	6. ???
1. Find Your Ground
	1. Forest Bathing
	2. Meditation in Nature
1. Visualize Safety and Relaxation
	1. Take a seat or lay down in a comfortable space where you won’t be interrupted
	2. Focus on breathing
	3. picture a white glowing light becoming stronger as you breathe
	4. As you begin to relax, feel the light fill your chest and repeat: “My heart is safe, I am safe. I am open and free.”
