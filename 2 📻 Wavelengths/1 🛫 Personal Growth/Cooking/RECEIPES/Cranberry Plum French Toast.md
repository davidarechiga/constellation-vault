**Cranberry Plum French Toast**
**Yield: 4 to 6 servings**
**For the Plums**
- [x] 2 cups cranberry juice
- [x] 1 cup sugar
- [x] 1 cinnamon stick
- [x] 2 pounds plums, halved and pitted
**For the French Toast**
- [x] 2 large eggs
- [x] ¼ cup milk
- [x] ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- [x] 1 tablespoon sugar
- [x] 2 tablespoons butter
- [x] 6 half-inch slices brioche
