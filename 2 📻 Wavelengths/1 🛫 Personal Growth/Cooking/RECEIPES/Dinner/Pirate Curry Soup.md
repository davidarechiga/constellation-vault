Here’s a simple Pirate Curry Soup recipe with 20oz of potatoes:

### Ingredients:
• 20oz potatoes (diced)
• 1 lb protein (shrimp, fish, or chicken)
• 1 onion (chopped)
• 2 cloves garlic (minced)
• 1 tbsp curry powder
• 1 tsp smoked paprika
• 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
• 1 can (14oz) coconut milk
• 4 cups broth (chicken or vegetable)
• 1 tbsp tomato paste
• 1 tbsp soy sauce
• 1 tbsp rum (optional, pirate touch!)
• 1 lime (juice)
• Salt & pepper to taste
• 2 tbsp oil
• Fresh cilantro & green onions for garnish

### Instructions:

1. Heat oil in a pot, sauté onions and garlic until soft.

2. Add curry powder, paprika, and cayenne. Stir for 30 sec.

3. Add tomato paste, soy sauce, and rum. Cook for 1 min.

4. Pour in broth and add potatoes. Simmer until tender (10-15 min).

5. Add protein of choice and coconut milk. Simmer until cooked (5-10 min).

6. Stir in lime juice. Adjust seasoning.

7. Serve hot, topped with cilantro and green onions. 

Pirate-approved!
