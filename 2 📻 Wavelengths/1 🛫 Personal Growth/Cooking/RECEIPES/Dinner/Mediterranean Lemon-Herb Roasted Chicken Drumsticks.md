With chicken drumsticks as your protein, let's create a delicious Mediterranean-inspired meal that will still pair beautifully with your tiramisu-style cannoli. This menu is designed to be impressive while allowing you to work gradually with your injured knee.

**Ingredients:**
- 1.75 lbs chicken drumsticks (about 6-8 drumsticks)
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 lemons (zest of both, juice of one)
- 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried)
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon honey
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Fresh parsley for garnish

**Instructions:**
1. **Day 1-2: Make the marinade**
   Combine olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, herbs, paprika, honey, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Mix well.

2. **Day 2: Marinate the chicken**
   Place chicken drumsticks in a zip-top bag or glass container. Pour marinade over chicken, ensuring all pieces are well-coated. Seal and refrigerate for 24-48 hours (this long marination will create incredible flavor).

3. **Day 4: Roasting**
   - Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
   - Remove chicken from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking to take the chill off.
   - Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or foil for easier cleanup.
   - Arrange drumsticks on the prepared sheet, leaving space between pieces.
   - Roast for 35-40 minutes, turning once halfway through, until skin is golden and crispy and internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
   - Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
   - Garnish with fresh parsley and lemon slices.

# Side Dish 1: Roasted Garlic and Herb Couscous

**Ingredients:**
- 1½ cups couscous
- 1¾ cups chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 head of garlic
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼ cup fresh herbs (parsley, mint, basil), chopped
- ¼ cup toasted pine nuts or slivered almonds
- ¼ cup dried cranberries or currants (optional)
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. **Day 3: Roast the garlic**
   - Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
   - Cut the top off a head of garlic to expose cloves.
   - Drizzle with olive oil, wrap in foil.
   - Roast for 30-35 minutes until soft and fragrant.
   - Cool and squeeze out roasted garlic cloves. Store in refrigerator.

2. **Day 4: Prepare couscous**
   - Bring broth to a boil in a medium saucepan.
   - Remove from heat, stir in couscous and a pinch of salt.
   - Cover and let stand for 5 minutes.
   - Fluff with a fork, then stir in roasted garlic, olive oil, herbs, nuts, and dried fruit.
   - Season with salt and pepper.

# Side Dish 2: Honey-Glazed Roasted Vegetables

**Ingredients:**
- 2 bell peppers (red and yellow), cut into chunks
- 1 medium zucchini, sliced into half-moons
- 1 medium red onion, cut into wedges
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. **Day 3: Prepare vegetables**
   - Wash and cut all vegetables. Store in separate containers in the refrigerator.
   - Mix olive oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper. Store in refrigerator.

2. **Day 4: Roast vegetables**
   - Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
   - Toss vegetables (except tomatoes) with the prepared dressing.
   - Spread on a baking sheet in a single layer.
   - Roast for 20 minutes, then add tomatoes.
   - Continue roasting for 10 more minutes until vegetables are caramelized and tender.
   - Top with crumbled feta if desired.

# Simple Cucumber-Yogurt Salad

**Ingredients:**
- 2 English cucumbers, thinly sliced
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh mint, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. **Day 3: Make dressing**
   - Mix yogurt, herbs, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Store in refrigerator.

2. **Day 4: Assemble salad**
   - Slice cucumbers thinly (you can do this earlier in the day).
   - Before serving, gently fold cucumbers into the yogurt mixture.
   - Chill until ready to serve.
