> [!info] 
> Crispy Outside, Cakey Inside - IBS-Conscious


> [!NOTE]
> Fills 8 loaf cavities (each 3.5 x 2.3 x 1.4 inches). Use muffin/donut molds for extra batter.

---
### Wet Ingredients
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain, lactose-free if needed)
- 1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil (or avocado oil)

---
### Dry Ingredients
- 1.5 cups oat flour (or finely ground quick oats)
- 1.5 cups whey protein (vanilla)
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg
- 2 tsp baking powder
- Pinch salt
- Optional: 1/4 cup golden raisins (soaked & drained)

---
### Topping (for crisp texture)
- 2 tbsp coconut sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Light spray of avocado oil

---
### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Mix wet ingredients until smooth.
3. Mix dry ingredients separately, then fold into wet.
4. Fold in raisins last.
5. Spray silicone molds lightly. Fill each cavity ~90%.
6. Sprinkle topping evenly on top.
7. Bake 20-25 min until golden edges and springy center.
8. Let cool in mold 10 min, then transfer to cooling rack.Plant-Based Protein Swaps
9. Reduce to 1 cup plant protein (they absorb more liquid).
10. Add 1/4 cup almond flour or more applesauce if batter feels dry.

---
#### Crispier Version Modifications
- Increase coconut sugar topping to 3 tbsp and broil last 2 min.
- Brush tops lightly with maple syrup + oil mixture after baking, then return to oven for 3-5 min at 375°F.

---
#### Chewier Version Modifications
- Use 1/2 cup mashed banana instead of applesauce.
- Add 1 tbsp ground flaxseed for extra chew.
- Bake 2-3 minutes less for softer center.
