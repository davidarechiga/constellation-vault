# Italian Chopped Sandwiches 

## Ingredients
- [ ] 4 hoagie or sub rolls 
- [ ] 2 cup shredded lettuce
- [ ] 6 slices of pepperoni
- [ ] 6 slices of salami
- [ ] 6 slices of ham
- [ ] 6 slices of tomato
- [ ] 6 slices of provolone cheese
- [ ] 1/4 cup banana peppers
- [ ] 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onions
- [ ] 1/4 cup mayonnaise
- [ ] 3 tablespoon olive oil
- [ ] 3 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried Italian seasoning
- [ ] Salt and pepper to taste.

## **INSTRUCTIONS**
1. Start with a base layer of shredded lettuce, then add your meats, cheese, tomatoes, banana peppers and red onions in a pile.
2. Chop up everything together, making sure it’s well mixed together.
3. Next add mayonnaise, olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
4. Chop one more time to incorporate.
5. Scoop a pile of the chopped Italian ingredients onto a hoagie roll and serve.
