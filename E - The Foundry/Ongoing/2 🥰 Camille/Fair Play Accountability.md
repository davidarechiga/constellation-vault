# Fair Play Accountability

> Owning a card means **Conception + Planning + Execution** — not just doing it when asked.
> Reference full CPE breakdown: [[Household Routines]]

---

## ✅ Daily Check-In (Every Morning)

Run through these in < 2 minutes:

- [ ] **Dishes** — sink clear? dishwasher loaded/unloaded?
- [ ] **Tidying** — common areas reset from last night?
- [ ] **Mail** — anything sitting unopened?
- [ ] **Pets (River)** — morning walk done?
- [ ] **Marriage & Romance** — did I connect with Camille yesterday? (not just logistics)

## 🔥 Minimum Standards of Care (MSC)

These are the baseline — if any are slipping, it's a signal to recalibrate:

- Sink clear before bed every night
- Dishwasher never left full and clean overnight
- No mail sits unopened 24+ hours
- Laundry folded and put away within a day of drying
- No bills paid late
- Weekend plan discussed with Camille before Saturday morning
- River walked daily
- Camille gets a genuine, non-logistical check-in daily

---

## 📊 Weekly Accountability Review

Every Sunday, ask yourself:

1. Which cards did I fully own this week (C + P + E)?
2. Which cards did I only execute when reminded?
3. Which cards did I drop entirely?
4. What's one thing Camille shouldn't have had to think about — but did?

> Honest answers here build trust faster than perfect performance.
