## Overview

River is our dog. Daily care, vet coordination, grooming, and training are all owned by David.

---

## Fair Play Card: Pets

> Full CPE ownership — I notice what River needs, plan for it, and execute. River's needs don't pause for life chaos.

- **Conception:** Track food level, vet schedule, grooming needs, behavioral patterns, boarding when we travel
- **Planning:** Schedule vet appointments and grooming; ensure food/supplies are stocked before running out; arrange boarding in advance
- **Execution:** Feed on schedule, walk daily, medicate if needed, take to vet, groom regularly

**MSC (Minimum Standard of Care):**
- River is fed on schedule every day — no exceptions
- At least one intentional walk or play session per day
- Vet appointments are kept and not rescheduled without reason
- Grooming happens on a regular cycle (not when it becomes obvious)
- Boarding/sitting is arranged well before any travel
