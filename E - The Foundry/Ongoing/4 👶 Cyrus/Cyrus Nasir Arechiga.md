## Fair Play Cards: Cyrus

> These are the cards I own for Cyrus. Full CPE ownership means I notice the need, plan for it, and execute — not just respond when asked.

### Diapering / Potty Training
- **Conception:** Track supply levels; notice readiness cues for potty training when the time comes
- **Planning:** Keep diapers, wipes, cream stocked before running out; research potty training approach
- **Execution:** Change promptly; lead the potty training process with consistency when ready
- **MSC:** Cyrus is never left in a dirty diaper. Supplies are stocked proactively. Potty training (when it starts) is patient and consistent.

### Grooming & Wardrobe
- **Conception:** Notice when Cyrus needs haircut, nail trim, new clothes, or outfit planning for events
- **Planning:** Schedule haircuts; assess wardrobe seasonally; shop ahead for growth spurts
- **Execution:** Haircuts, nail trims, bathing, assembling outfits for events
- **MSC:** Cyrus looks cared-for. Nails don't get uncomfortably long. Wardrobe reviewed each season — not scrambling the morning of an event.

### Watching (Kids) — My Coverage Shifts
- **Conception:** Know the weekly childcare schedule; notice gaps, sick days, or coverage needs
- **Planning:** Coordinate with Camille on schedule; line up backup coverage proactively
- **Execution:** Be genuinely present when it's my watch; arrange coverage when unavailable (24hr notice minimum)
- **MSC:** Cyrus is never without a caregiver. "My watch" = fully present, not half-present. Coverage gaps communicated to Camille in advance.
