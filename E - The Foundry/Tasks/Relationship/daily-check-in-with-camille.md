# Daily 15-Min Check-In with Camille

**Frequency:** Every evening (7:00-7:15 PM recommended)
**Duration:** 15 minutes minimum

## Check-In Structure

1. **Family Handshake** (ritual opener)
2. **Review [[Daily Check-in Questions]]**
   - What was positive about today?
   - How are you feeling emotionally?
   - What do you need from me?
   - How can we communicate better?
   - Did we spend quality time today?
3. **Share gratitude** or highlight from the day
4. **Discuss tomorrow** - coordinate schedule and tasks

**Why matters:** Daily connection prevents resentment, maintains emotional intimacy, and keeps both partners aligned - especially critical with baby arriving soon. This practice is one of Camille's most valued relationship rituals.

**Done when:** Both feel heard, tomorrow is coordinated, and connection is reaffirmed.

## Tips for Successful Check-Ins

✅ **DO**:
- Put phones away completely
- Make eye contact
- Listen without interrupting
- Validate feelings ("That makes sense", "I hear you")
- Take notes if action items emerge

❌ **DON'T**:
- Multitask during check-in
- Dismiss concerns or feelings
- Rush through questions
- Make it about solving problems (sometimes just listen)
- Skip if tired (this is when it matters most)
