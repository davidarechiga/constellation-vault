# Auto

> Always registered, insured, and maintained. Warning lights addressed within a week.

**Frequency:** Monthly glance + action as triggered

---

## Monthly Check (1st of month)

- [ ] Oil change due? (every 5,000–7,500 miles depending on vehicle)
- [ ] Any warning lights on dashboard?
- [ ] Tires look normal? (pressure, tread)
- [ ] Registration renewal coming up in next 60 days?
- [ ] Insurance renewal coming up?

## Annual Tasks

- [ ] Renew registration before expiration
- [ ] Review auto insurance — coverage still right? better rate available?
- [ ] Schedule smog check if required
- [ ] Rotate tires (every 6,000–8,000 miles)

## As-Needed

- [ ] Warning light appears → address within 1 week
- [ ] Accident or damage → document, contact insurance, arrange repair
- [ ] Car seat safety check (with Cyrus) — inspect installation periodically
