# Health Insurance

> Both partners know our coverage. No claim goes unsubmitted. Open enrollment never sneaks up.

**Frequency:** Monthly check (1st of month) + action as needed

---

## Monthly Check

- [ ] Any new medical visits to submit claims for?
- [ ] Any EOBs (Explanation of Benefits) received — reviewed and filed?
- [ ] Any prior authorizations pending?
- [ ] Camille and Cyrus covered under current plan?

## Open Enrollment (Annual — typically Nov)

- [ ] Review current plan coverage and costs
- [ ] Compare plan options if available
- [ ] Confirm all family members enrolled (David, Camille, Cyrus)
- [ ] Update FSA/HSA contributions if applicable
- [ ] Confirm enrollment submitted before deadline

## Reference Info

- [ ] Know our deductible and out-of-pocket max
- [ ] Know which providers are in-network
- [ ] Know how to submit a claim manually
- [ ] Camille knows where to find insurance cards

---

**Why matters:** With Cyrus here, medical visits are frequent. Missed claims or coverage gaps cost real money and create real stress.

**Done when:** All claims submitted, both partners know coverage details, open enrollment handled before deadline.
