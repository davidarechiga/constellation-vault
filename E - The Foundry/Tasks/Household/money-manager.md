# Money Manager

> Full CPE ownership — I know the financial picture completely, not just pay bills when they arrive.

**Frequency:** Monthly deep review (1st of month) + weekly pulse check (Tuesdays)

---

## Monthly Review Checklist

- [ ] Review last month's spending — any surprises or overruns?
- [ ] Check all account balances (checking, savings, credit cards)
- [ ] Confirm all bills were paid — nothing missed or late
- [ ] Review autopay subscriptions — anything to cancel or change?
- [ ] Check savings progress toward current goals
- [ ] Reconcile any large purchases from last month
- [ ] Update Camille on financial picture — one shared awareness moment

## Weekly Pulse (Tuesdays — < 5 min)

- [ ] Any bills due this week?
- [ ] Credit card balance looking normal?
- [ ] Any unexpected charges to investigate?

## Annual Tasks

- [ ] File taxes (or confirm with accountant)
- [ ] Review insurance coverage (home, renters, life)
- [ ] Audit subscriptions annually (January)
- [ ] Update beneficiaries if anything changed

---

**Why matters:** Financial surprises are stress multipliers — especially with a new baby. Owning this card fully means Camille never has to wonder if something got paid.

**Done when:** All bills current, budget reviewed, Camille has monthly financial awareness, no surprise overdrafts or late fees.
