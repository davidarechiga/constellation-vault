# Birthday Celebrations (Cyrus)

> Planning starts 4–6 weeks out. Cyrus feels special on his birthday.

**Rule:** If Cyrus's birthday is within 6 weeks, it's already time to start.

---

## 6 Weeks Out

- [ ] Decide on birthday theme or vibe (ask Cyrus when old enough)
- [ ] Guest list — family only? friends? small or big?
- [ ] Venue decision — home, park, venue?
- [ ] Budget set

## 4 Weeks Out

- [ ] Invitations sent (if applicable)
- [ ] Cake or dessert planned and ordered/sourced
- [ ] Activities or entertainment planned
- [ ] Gift ideas researched — what does Cyrus actually want/need?

## 2 Weeks Out

- [ ] Decorations sourced or ordered
- [ ] Gifts purchased (not week-of)
- [ ] Food/snacks planned and shopped for (or catering arranged)
- [ ] Day-of logistics mapped out

## Week Of

- [ ] Setup plan confirmed
- [ ] Camille's vision for the day synced
- [ ] Cyrus knows something special is coming

## Day Of

- [ ] Make Cyrus feel genuinely celebrated
- [ ] Document — photos and a note about this year

---

**Why matters:** Birthdays are anchor memories. Cyrus deserves a dad who plans ahead, not one scrambling the night before.

**Done when:** Cyrus has a celebration that feels intentional and joyful, gifts purchased in advance, Camille didn't have to drive the planning.
