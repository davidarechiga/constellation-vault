# Research 5 Target Companies

## Company Research Process (For Each Company)

- [ ] Identify company name and website
- [ ] Research company mission and values
- [ ] Review careers page and job openings
- [ ] Check Glassdoor reviews (culture, work-life balance, salary)
- [ ] Look up company on LinkedIn (employee count, growth, recent hires)
- [ ] Find hiring managers or design/engineering leads on LinkedIn
- [ ] Check if company has remote/hybrid policy
- [ ] Research company's tech stack (React, TypeScript, etc.?)
- [ ] Assess company stage (startup, growth, established, enterprise)

## Selection Criteria

Choose companies that align with your values:
- **Work-life balance** - Critical with newborn
- **Remote flexibility** - Need hybrid or full remote
- **Design + dev hybrid culture** - Values Creative Technologists
- **Interesting product/mission** - Something you'd be excited to build
- **Reasonable compensation** - Meets salary requirements

## Research Resources

### Company Information
- **Company Website** - Mission, values, team, blog
- **LinkedIn Company Page** - Size, growth, recent hires, employee posts
- **Glassdoor** - Reviews, salary data, interview experiences
- **Blind** - Anonymous employee discussions (tech company culture)
- **Crunchbase** - Funding, investors, growth stage (for startups)

### Tech Stack
- **BuiltWith** - See what technologies companies use
- **StackShare** - Engineering blog tech stack disclosures
- **Company engineering blogs** - Often detail technical decisions

### Culture & Values
- **Glassdoor reviews** - Read 10-15 recent reviews, look for patterns
- **LinkedIn employee posts** - What do employees share publicly?
- **Company blog** - Tone, values, how they treat employees
- **Diversity reports** - Do they publish DEI data?

---

## Red Flags to Watch For

🚩 **Poor work-life balance**
- Glassdoor reviews mention long hours, burnout
- "We work hard, play hard" culture (often means overwork)

🚩 **Toxic culture**
- High turnover (check LinkedIn - employees leaving quickly)
- Negative Glassdoor reviews about management

🚩 **Unclear remote policy**
- Forcing RTO (return to office) during/after pandemic
- Vague about remote work expectations

🚩 **Unrealistic expectations**
- Job descriptions requiring 5+ years in tools that are 2 years old
- "Looking for a rockstar ninja unicorn" language

---

## Ideal Company Characteristics

✅ **Strong work-life balance** (4+ rating on Glassdoor)
✅ **Remote-first or hybrid** with clear policy
✅ **Values design + engineering** (has Creative Technologists or Design Engineers)
✅ **Reasonable interview process** (not 8-round marathon)
✅ **Transparent compensation** (lists salary ranges in job posts)
✅ **Healthy growth** (not chaotic hypergrowth, not stagnant)
✅ **Supportive of parents** (generous parental leave, flexible schedules)
