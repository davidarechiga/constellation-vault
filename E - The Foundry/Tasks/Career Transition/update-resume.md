## Resume Format Tips

**Summary Section:**
Position yourself as Creative Technologist (design + dev hybrid):
> "Creative Technologist with expertise in React, TypeScript, and design systems. Bridges design and engineering by translating visual concepts into polished, performant web applications. Strong foundation in both UI/UX design (Figma) and modern frontend development."

**Experience Section:**
- Use action verbs (Built, Designed, Implemented, Led, Created)
- Quantify impact where possible (improved performance by X%, built feature used by Y users)
- Focus on relevant projects for Creative Technologist role

**Skills Section:**
Group by category:
- **Frontend Development**: React, TypeScript, Canvas API, Zustand
- **Design Tools**: Figma, Adobe Creative Suite
- **Development Tools**: Git, VS Code, Chrome DevTools
