# Get Professional Headshots

## Research & Book
- [ ] Research local photographers (headshot specialists)
- [ ] Check portfolios and pricing
- [ ] Read reviews
- [ ] Book photography session
- [ ] Confirm date, time, location
- [ ] Pay deposit if required

## Prepare for Session
- [ ] Choose 2-3 outfit options (solid colors, business casual)
- [ ] Avoid busy patterns or logos
- [ ] Get haircut 3-5 days before shoot (not day-of)
- [ ] Get good sleep night before
- [ ] Prepare confident, approachable expression

## Day of Shoot
- [ ] Arrive on time with outfits
- [ ] Take multiple shots with different expressions
- [ ] Try different angles and poses
- [ ] Review shots during session
- [ ] Request variety (smiling, neutral, professional)

## Post-Shoot
- [ ] Review proof gallery from photographer
- [ ] Select 2-3 final images
- [ ] Receive high-resolution files
- [ ] Update LinkedIn profile photo
- [ ] Update resume header photo
- [ ] Update professional email signature

**Why matters**: Professional headshot establishes credibility and makes strong first impression on LinkedIn, resume, and applications. Critical component of professional brand for Creative Technologist role.

**Done when**: Professional headshots taken, final images selected and received, and photos updated across LinkedIn, resume, and email signature.

## Headshot Style Guide

### For Creative Technologist Role

**Vibe**: Professional but approachable, creative but polished

**Outfit Recommendations**:
- **Option 1**: Dark button-down shirt (navy, charcoal, black)
- **Option 2**: Casual blazer with solid-color t-shirt
- **Option 3**: Well-fitted sweater or henley

**Avoid**:
- Busy patterns, stripes, plaids
- Logos or branding
- All white or all black (hard to photograph)
- Wrinkled or ill-fitting clothes

**Background**:
- Neutral (gray, white, light blue)
- Slight blur for depth
- Nothing distracting

**Expression**:
- Warm, confident smile (approachable)
- Neutral professional (focused, competent)
- Try both and choose based on feel

## Finding Photographers

**Search Terms**:
- "Professional headshots Bay Area"
- "LinkedIn headshots Berkeley/Oakland"
- "Corporate photography San Francisco"

**Price Range**: $150-$400 for headshot session

**What's Included** (verify before booking):
- Number of final edited images (aim for 2-3)
- Turnaround time (typically 3-7 days)
- High-resolution files for web and print
- Usage rights (should be yours to use freely)

---

## Photo Specs for Different Platforms

**LinkedIn**: 400 x 400 px minimum (JPG or PNG)
**Resume Header**: 150 x 150 px to 300 x 300 px
**Email Signature**: 150 x 150 px (keep file size small for email)

Photographer should provide files that can be resized for all uses.
