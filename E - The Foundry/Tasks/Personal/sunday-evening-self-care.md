# Sunday Evening Self-Care Ritual

**Frequency:** Every Sunday evening
**Time:** 7:00-8:30 PM
**Duration:** 60-90 minutes

## Weekly Self-Care Checklist

- [ ] **Massage / Self-massage** (15-20 min)
  - Use massage gun or foam roller
  - Focus on tension areas (shoulders, back, legs)
  - Slow, intentional movements
- [ ] **Personal grooming** (15-20 min)
  - Clip nails (fingers and toes)
  - Shave (if desired)
  - Skin care routine
- [ ] **Shower / Bath** (15-20 min)
  - Warm, relaxing shower
  - Intentional, not rushed
  - Use nice soap/products
- [ ] **Meditation** (10-15 min)
  - Guided or silent meditation
  - Mindfulness breathing practice
  - Body scan relaxation
- [ ] **Journaling** (5-10 min)
  - Reflect on the week
  - What went well? What was challenging?
  - Intentions for the coming week
- [ ] **Reading** (10-15 min)
  - Fiction or personal development
  - No screens, physical book
  - Something enjoyable and calming

**Why matters:** Sunday evening ritual creates clear boundary between weekend and work week. Self-care reduces stress, prevents burnout, and models healthy habits (important as new parent). Weekly reset supports mental and physical wellness.

**Done when:** All elements completed, feel refreshed and ready for the week ahead, tension released and intention set.

## Environment Setup

**Create relaxing atmosphere:**
- Dim lights or use candles
- Play calming music (ambient, instrumental, nature sounds)
- Silence phone notifications
- Tell Camille you're taking self-care time
- Close bathroom/bedroom door for privacy
- Set comfortable temperature

**Supplies to have ready:**
- Massage gun or foam roller
- Nail clippers, file
- Shaving supplies (if shaving)
- Nice soap, shampoo, lotion
- Clean towels
- Journal and pen
- Book currently reading
- Meditation app or timer

---

## Flexibility & Adaptation

**Minimum viable ritual** (if time-limited - 30 min):
- Shower (10 min)
- Clip nails (5 min)
- Brief meditation (10 min)
- Quick journaling (5 min)

**Extended ritual** (if extra time - 2+ hours):
- Add bath instead of shower
- Longer meditation (20-30 min)
- Extended reading time
- Stretching or yoga
- Face mask or spa treatment

---

## Journaling Prompts

**Weekly reflection questions:**

1. **What am I grateful for this week?**
2. **What challenged me? How did I respond?**
3. **What did I learn about myself?**
4. **Where did I show up well?**
5. **Where can I improve next week?**
6. **What's one intention for the coming week?**
7. **How am I feeling physically and emotionally?**

Keep prompts simple - just 2-3 sentences per question.

---

## Tips for Consistency

✅ **Success Strategies:**
- Block time on calendar (treat as appointment)
- Tell Camille this is your weekly self-care time
- Prepare supplies Saturday so ready Sunday
- Start ritual same time each week
- Track in habit tracker for accountability
- Give yourself permission to take this time

❌ **Common Pitfalls:**
- Skipping when tired (when you need it most)
- Rushing through steps
- Checking phone during ritual
- Letting others interrupt
- Feeling guilty for taking time for self

**Remember:** Taking care of yourself enables you to take care of others (especially with baby arriving). Self-care is not selfish.
