# Hospital Pre-Registration

- [ ] Access Alta Bates patient portal
- [ ] Complete pre-registration form online
- [ ] Upload insurance information (ID, card images)
- [ ] Verify contact information and emergency contacts
- [ ] Print confirmation of pre-registration
- [ ] Bring confirmation to hospital bag

**Why critical**: Pre-registering saves time and reduces stress during labor admission. Completing this by Week 37 (Feb 15) ensures readiness for early arrival.

**Done when**: Pre-registration form submitted through Alta Bates patient portal, confirmation received, and printed copy added to hospital bag documents.

**Related**: [[1 Baby Prep - Cyrus]] • [[Hospital Bag Checklist]] • [[Childbirth Intensive Alta Bates]]

---

## How to Pre-Register

From Alta Bates class notes:
> "You can do this through your patient portal or by going on the website for the hospital you are going. This saves a lot of questions when you come in during labor."

## Alta Bates Information

**Hospital**: Alta Bates Summit Medical Center
**Labor & Delivery**: 2450 Ashby Avenue, Berkeley, CA
**Phone**: 510-204-4461 (Parent Education Office)

## Registration Methods

1. **Patient Portal** - Log in to Alta Bates patient portal and find pre-registration link
2. **Hospital Website** - Visit Alta Bates website and look for "Pre-Register for Delivery"
3. **Phone Registration** - Call Parent Education Office if online not available

## Documents Needed

- Insurance card (front and back)
- ID (driver's license or passport)
- Pediatrician contact info (if selected)
- Emergency contact information
- Birth plan or preferences worksheet (optional to upload)

## After Pre-Registration

- Add printed confirmation to hospital bag ([[Hospital Bag Checklist]])
- Know hospital parking location and entrance to use
- Save hospital phone number in phone contacts
