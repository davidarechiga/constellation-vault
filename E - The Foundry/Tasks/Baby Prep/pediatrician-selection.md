# Pediatrician Selection

- [ ] Get referrals from friends, doula, or OB
- [ ] Research pediatricians in network (verify insurance coverage)
- [ ] Check office locations and hours
- [ ] Read online reviews and check credentials
- [ ] Call top 2-3 practices to ask interview questions
- [ ] Schedule "meet the pediatrician" appointment (if offered)
- [ ] Choose pediatrician and provide contact to hospital
- [ ] Add pediatrician info to hospital bag documents

**Why matters**: Hospital will contact your chosen pediatrician after baby is born for newborn examination and discharge clearance. Must be selected and communicated to Alta Bates before delivery (Week 38 - Feb 22).

**Done when**: Pediatrician selected, practice contacted and confirmed they accept your insurance, contact information added to hospital pre-registration and hospital bag documents.

**Related**: [[1 Baby Prep - Cyrus]] • [[Hospital Bag Checklist]]

## Interview Questions to Ask

When calling pediatrician offices:

**Logistics:**
- Do you have privileges at Alta Bates Hospital?
- What are your office hours?
- How do you handle after-hours emergencies?
- What is your sick visit policy?
- How quickly can we get same-day appointments?

**Philosophy:**
- What is your approach to vaccination schedules?
- How do you support breastfeeding parents?
- Do you offer lactation consultant referrals?
- What is your stance on alternative medicine/complementary care?

**Practice:**
- How many pediatricians are in the practice?
- Will we always see the same doctor?
- What hospital are you affiliated with?
- Do you offer virtual/telemedicine appointments?

## From Alta Bates Class Notes

> "Interview & choose a doctor for your baby. This is something you do before birth. When your baby or babies are born, we will contact the practice and if they have privileges at Alta Bates they can come and examine and discharge the baby. If they do not have privileges, a staff pediatrician will do this and you will see someone from your own practice during the first week you are home from the hospital."

## Important Timing

- **Choose by Week 38** (Feb 22, 2026)
- **Provide contact to hospital** during pre-registration
- **First appointment** typically scheduled for 3-5 days after birth
