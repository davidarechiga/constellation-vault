## Pro Tips

- **Install 2-3 weeks early** - Allows time for adjustments or professional inspection
- **Inch test**: Grab base at belt path and try to move side-to-side. Should move less than 1 inch.
- **Harness test**: Should fit snugly on doll/baby with no slack
- **Chest clip position**: At armpit level
- **Recline angle**: Check indicator on car seat base (usually 30-45 degrees for newborns)
