## Safe Sleep Guidelines (SIDS Prevention)

From Alta Bates class:
> "Baby will be sleeping in the same room with the parents as a part of SIDS prevention"

**Safe Sleep Checklist:**
- Firm, flat mattress (no soft bedding, pillows, bumpers)
- Fitted sheet only (nothing else in sleep space)
- Baby on back to sleep (never stomach or side)
- Room sharing but not bed sharing
- Smoke-free environment
- Temperature-controlled room (not too hot)

## Pro Tips

- **Wash everything** - All clothes, sheets, blankets before baby arrives (fragrance-free detergent)
- **Staging stations** - Set up multiple changing stations (nursery, living room) for convenience
- **Night setup** - Test night light brightness, ensure everything needed is within arm's reach
- **Accessibility** - Keep most-used items at eye level and easy to grab with one hand
