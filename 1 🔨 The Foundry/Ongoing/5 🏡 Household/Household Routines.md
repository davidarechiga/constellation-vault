# Household Routines

> **Quick Access:** [[5 🏡 Household]]

## Weekly Tasks

### Waste Management
  - Check all bathroom trash cans
  - Collect kitchen trash and recycling
  - Take bins to curb on Wednesday evening
  - Bring bins back after pickup Thursday morning
  - **Done when**: All trash cans emptied and bins returned from curb

## Monthly Tasks

### Deep Cleaning Rotation

## Before Baby Arrival

**Target completion: February 22, 2026**

### Kitchen Deep Clean
  - Clean inside microwave, oven, and refrigerator
  - Wipe down all cabinet fronts and handles
  - Organize pantry and discard expired items
  - Deep clean sink and faucet (remove hard water stains)
  - Mop floor and baseboards
  - **Done when**: Kitchen is guest-ready and fully sanitized for baby prep

### Bathroom Deep Clean
  - Scrub tub, shower, and tile grout
  - Clean toilet thoroughly (including behind and under)
  - Wipe down all surfaces, mirrors, and fixtures
  - Organize under-sink storage
  - Wash bath mats and shower curtain
  - Restock toiletries and supplies
  - **Done when**: Bathroom is spotless and ready for postpartum recovery

### Dining Room Deep Clean
  - Dust light fixture and ceiling fan
  - Clean table and chairs thoroughly
  - Vacuum or mop floor
  - Wipe down baseboards and window sills
  - Organize any clutter or storage items
  - **Done when**: Space is clear and welcoming for family meals

### Entry Way Deep Clean
  - Organize shoe storage
  - Clean front door and hardware
  - Sweep/mop floor
  - Dust any decorative items
  - Create designated spot for diaper bag and baby gear
  - **Done when**: Entryway is organized and ready for quick exits with baby

---

## Notes

**Purpose**: These routines help maintain a clean, organized home environment as we prepare for Cyrus's arrival and adjust to life with a newborn.

**Priority Adjustment**: Deep cleaning tasks are high priority before baby arrives, then shift to maintenance mode afterward.
