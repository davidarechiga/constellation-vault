## Brand Context

From Brand Aesthetic Guidelines:

**Visual Style:**
- Grid systems and pixel illustrations
- Geometric shapes with clean lines
- Texture overlays and digital artifacts
- Intentional glitch effects
- Minimalist with moments of chaos

**Color Palette:**
- Deep blues, electric purples, vibrant greens
- Warm ambers, cyan & magenta accents
- High contrast, bold combinations

**Typography:**
- VCR OSD Mono (retro digital feel)
- Space Grotesk (modern geometric)
- JetBrains Mono (code/technical)

**Logo Concept:**
- Minimalist geometric mark
- Optical illusion or visual interest
- Two states: "stable" (clean) and "glitched" (disrupted)

---

## Logo Design Principles

**Keep it simple:**
- Should work at 16x16 pixels (favicon)
- Recognizable at large and small sizes
- Works in one color (black or white)

**Memorable:**
- Distinctive shape or form
- Not derivative of other brands
- Reflects Pixel Glitch aesthetic

**Versatile:**
- Works on any background
- Scales up and down
- Animated and static versions

**Timeless (but with edge):**
- Avoid trendy effects that date quickly
- Intentional glitch (not random corruption)
- Balance between clean and chaotic
