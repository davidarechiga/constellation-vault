# Weekly Waste Management

**Frequency:** Every Wednesday evening
**Duration:** 15-20 minutes
**Trash Pickup:** Thursday morning

## Weekly Checklist

- [ ] Check all bathroom trash cans and empty
- [ ] Collect kitchen trash and recycling
- [ ] Take bins to curb on Wednesday evening (after 6pm)
- [ ] Bring bins back from curb Thursday after pickup (after 10am)

**Why matters:** Trash pickup is Thursday morning - bins must go out Wednesday night. Keeps home clean and prevents overflow. Simple routine task that supports household functioning.

**Done when:** All trash cans emptied, bins placed at curb Wednesday evening, and bins returned Thursday after pickup.

**Related:** [[Household Routines]] • [[5 🏡 Household]]

---

## Detailed Routine

### Wednesday Evening (6:00-6:20 PM)

**Step 1: Bathroom Trash (5 min)**
- Check master bathroom trash
- Check guest bathroom trash
- Empty small bins into main trash bag

**Step 2: Kitchen Collection (5 min)**
- Remove full trash bag from kitchen can
- Collect recycling from kitchen bin
- Check under sink for additional trash

**Step 3: Roll Bins to Curb (5 min)**
- Roll gray trash bin to curb
- Roll blue recycling bin to curb
- Position at street edge (not blocking sidewalk/driveway)

### Thursday Morning (After 10:00 AM)

**Step 4: Return Bins (5 min)**
- Wait until after trash pickup (usually 8-10am)
- Roll empty bins back from curb
- Position bins in garage or designated spot

## Tips

- **Set Wednesday reminder** - Phone alarm at 6:00 PM
- **Check weather** - If raining, consider moving bins under eave
- **Night before prep** - Collect trash Wednesday rather than waiting until evening
- **Combine with other tasks** - Do on way to/from dog walk
