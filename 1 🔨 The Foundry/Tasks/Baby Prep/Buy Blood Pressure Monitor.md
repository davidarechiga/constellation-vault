## Research: Pregnancy-Validated Blood Pressure Monitors

**⚠️ CRITICAL**: Most home BP monitors are NOT validated for pregnancy. Only use devices specifically validated for pregnancy/preeclampsia monitoring.

### Top Pregnancy-Validated Options (2026)

1. **Preeclampsia Foundation Cuff Kit** ⭐ Recommended
   - Uses iHealth Track device (pregnancy-validated)
   - Includes evidence-based education materials
   - Available through preeclampsia.org/order-cuff-kits
   - May be covered by insurance or provided by OB

2. **Microlife Monitors** (All validated for pregnancy)
   - Microlife 3BTO-A
   - Microlife WatchBP Home
   - All Microlife upper-arm monitors validated for preeclampsia

3. **Omron Monitors** (Select models validated)
   - Omron M7
   - Omron MIT
   - Omron MIT Elite
   - NOTE: Not all Omron models are pregnancy-validated - verify before purchase

### General Top-Rated Monitors (2026)
- **A&D Medical Premium Multi-User** - Perfect score across all categories
- **Omron 3 Series** - Strong performance, budget-friendly
- **Braun ExactFit 2** - AMA clinically validated

### Key Features to Look For
- ✅ **Pregnancy validation** (most important)
- ✅ **Upper arm cuff** (not wrist or finger)
- ✅ **Irregular heartbeat detection**
- ✅ **Memory storage** for tracking trends
- ✅ **Large, easy-to-read display**
- ✅ **Proper cuff size** (measure upper arm circumference)

### Proper Use Guidelines
- Sit quietly for 5 minutes before taking reading
- Arm at heart level, back supported, feet flat
- No talking during measurement
- Take 2-3 readings, 1-2 minutes apart
- Same time each day for consistency
- Track readings to share with OB

### Warning Signs (Call OB Immediately)
- Systolic ≥140 or Diastolic ≥90 (on two occasions, 4+ hours apart)
- Sudden spike in blood pressure
- BP elevation + severe headache, vision changes, or upper abdominal pain
