# Pack Hospital Bag

## Packing Strategy

**Small Carry-On:** Mom's clothes, baby's clothes, partner's clothes, all chargers
**Blue Duffel:** Documents, labor items, toiletries, nursing supplies, extra comfort items
**Empty Duffel:** To bring home hospital gifts, samples, and supplies

**Hospital provides:** Diapers, wipes, basic toiletries, pads, disposable underwear, peri bottle, ice packs

- **Why critical**: Hospital requires these items for discharge, and having everything prepared reduces stress during labor. Pack by Week 36 (February 15, 2026) as recommended by Alta Bates childbirth class.

- **Done when**: All three bags packed and staged by front door; car seat installed and tested; birth plan copies printed; pediatrician contact info confirmed

- **Related**: [[Hospital Bag Checklist]] • [[Childbirth Intensive Alta Bates]] • [[1 Baby Prep - Cyrus]]

- **Pro tip**: Use two bags - small carry-on for essentials (clothes, chargers) and larger duffel for everything else. Bring an empty bag to carry home hospital supplies and gifts.
