## Overview

A 9-week mindfulness-based childbirth and parenting program (MBCP) designed to cultivate awareness, reduce stress, and prepare for labor, birth, and early parenting through meditation practices and evidence-based education.

### Goals
- Develop a consistent mindfulness meditation practice
- Learn techniques for managing pain and stress during labor
- Build confidence for the birthing experience

## Key Concepts

### The 9 Attitudes of Mindfulness (Jon Kabat-Zinn)
1. **Beginner's Mind** - Approaching each moment with fresh eyes
2. **Non-judging** - Observing without labeling as good or bad
3. **Acceptance** - Acknowledging things as they are
4. **Letting Go** - Releasing attachment to outcomes
5. **Trust** - Trusting your body and the process
6. **Patience** - Allowing things to unfold in their own time
7. **Non-striving** - Being present without forcing goals
8. **Gratitude** - Appreciating what is
9. **Generosity** - Giving presence and kindness

### Core Practices
- **Awareness of Breathing** - Foundation meditation practice
- **Body Scan** - Full-body awareness meditation
- **Mindful Eating** - The raisin exercise
- **Mindful Movement** - Gentle yoga and stretching
