## **Down By The River**
**Ingredients:**
- 2oz Tequila
- 1oz cranberry juice
- 1oz of grapefruit juice 
- 1oz of agave syrup
- Ginger Beer
**Instructions:**
Combine and shake the ingredients and pour into a highball glass and top with Ginger Beer

## **We Met In The City**
### Ingredients
- 2 ounces dry gin
- 1 ounce lemon juice
- 3/4 ounce honey syrup
### Instructions:
Shake all ingredients with ice, then Strain into a chilled coupe and garnish with lemon twist
