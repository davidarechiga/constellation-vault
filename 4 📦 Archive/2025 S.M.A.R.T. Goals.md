### Clear Mind
#### Morning Routine (60 minutes)

**Specific**: Establish a morning routine that includes reading a daily reminder note, going through tasks, journaling, and exercising.

**Measurable**: Complete the routine 5 days a week.

**Achievable**: Allocate 60 minutes each morning, adjusting the time for other activities as needed.

**Relevant**: A consistent morning routine will enhance focus and organization.

**Time-bound**: Start by the end of January 2025 and aim for consistency by March 2025.
