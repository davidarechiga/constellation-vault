## Major Philsophical Issues in Psychology
- scientific approach assumes we live in a universe of cause and effect
- Free will vs Determinism
	- Determinism: the idea that everything that happens has a cause or determinant that can be observed or measured
	- Free will: the belief that behavior is caused by a person’s independent decisions
- Mind-Brain Problem
	- Philosophical question of how experience relates to the brain
	- Dualism: mind is separate from the brain but somehow
	- Monism: the view that conscious experience is inseparable from the brain
- Nature-Nurture Issue
	- How do differences in behavior relate to differences in heredity and environment?

## What Psychologists Do
