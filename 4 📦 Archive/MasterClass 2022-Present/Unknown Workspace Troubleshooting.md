# Unknown Workspace Troubleshooting

![[20007592930.png]]

1. Change computers
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Access in a different browser
4. Access in incognito mode
5. Access [ourmasterclass.slack.com](http://ourmasterclass.slack.com)
6. Update Broswer
7. Log out of OneLogin, close out Chrome, relaunch Chrome and sign back into OneLogin again.
8. reboot the computer
9. test a different network if possible
10. Have the support team check the firewall settings
11. Make sure the timezone/time is correct on the desktop (even if it's a minute off, this can cause issues)
12. Lastly, sign out of Onelogin a couple times as this has worked for users in the past.
