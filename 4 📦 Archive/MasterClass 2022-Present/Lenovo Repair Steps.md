# Lenovo Repair Steps

Once a computer is determined to need a physical repair, proceed with these steps:

1. Navigate to https://support.lenovo.com/us/en/servicerequest
2. Enter the Serial Number for the Device.
3. Enter more details about the issue and troubleshooting steps.
4. Review Submission.
5. Submission Received.
6. Lenovo will send a confirmation email.
7. Lenovo will send a box to you.
8. Send the Laptop to a Lenovo Repair Center.
9. They send back the laptop.
10. Function Test repaired Laptop. This includes: Turning on the machine, testing the Speaker, microphone, wifi, and camera.
11. Enroll in Autopilot/Intune if not enrolled yet.
