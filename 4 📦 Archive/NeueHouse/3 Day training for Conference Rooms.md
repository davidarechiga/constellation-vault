# 3 Day training for Conference Rooms

Day 1: Training

- &nbsp;
	- Going over conference room tech
		- Airplay (30min)
		- Video Conferencing (up to 60min)
		- Printing (30min)
Day 2: Shadowing

- &nbsp;
	- Having trainee observe steps
Day 3: Reverse Shadowing

- &nbsp;
	- Observing Trainee and providing feedback
Day 4: Testing Trainee

- &nbsp;
	- Test a Live (staged or actual) conference room booking
