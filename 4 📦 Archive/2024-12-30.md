#### Controlling triggers
- **Mindfulness** — Regular mindfulness practice helps in recognizing and distancing oneself from impulsive urges.
- **Inventory Triggers** — Keeping a list of situations that trigger impulsivity can help in avoiding or managing them.
- **Substance Avoidance** — Reducing or eliminating alcohol and drug use can help maintain better impulse control.
- **Set Boundaries** — Establishing personal limits and sticking to them can prevent impulsive actions.
- **Alternative Outlets** — Finding healthier outlets for impulsive energy, such as [exercise](about:blank?type=ask-arc&q=exercise&unit_title=Alternative%20Outlets&unit_subtitle=Finding%20healthier%20outlets%20for%20impulsive%20energy,%20such%20as%20exercise%20or%20creative%20activities,%20can%20be%20beneficial.&context=activity) or [creative activities](about:blank?type=ask-arc&q=creative%20activities&unit_title=Alternative%20Outlets&unit_subtitle=Finding%20healthier%20outlets%20for%20impulsive%20energy,%20such%20as%20exercise%20or%20creative%20activities,%20can%20be%20beneficial.&context=activity), can be beneficial.
